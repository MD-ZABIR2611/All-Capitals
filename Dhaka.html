About Dhaka

Dhaka, the vibrant capital of Bangladesh, stands as one of the most densely populated and dynamic cities in the world. Located on the banks of the Buriganga River, it blends a rich Mughal heritage with modern urbanization.

From the 17th century as the capital of Bengal under the Mughals to becoming the political, economic, and cultural heart of modern Bangladesh, Dhaka pulses with energy, color, and resilience.

Culture & Cuisine

🍛

Street Food Heaven

From fuchka (pani puri) to chotpoti, biryani from Old Dhaka, and sweet chai — Dhaka's food scene is legendary.

🚲

Rickshaw Art

Colorful, hand-painted rickshaws are a moving canvas — representing folk art, cinema heroes, and daily life.

🕌

Festivals

Pohela Boishakh (Bengali New Year), Durga Puja, and Eid bring the city alive with processions and celebrations.

🎨

Jamdani Weaving

UNESCO-listed handloom tradition — intricate muslin textiles that represent Dhaka's artistic soul.
